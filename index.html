About The SPARC

Student Professional Awareness Regional Conclave, popularly known as SPARC is the third edition of the award-winning national-level flagship event of IEEE MACE SB, earlier branded as SPAAM. A three-day-long offline event, SPARC is a complete package that comprises inspirational talk sessions, engaging workshops and many more exuberant activities that are focused on imparting professional awareness to aspiring minds along with developing their technical and soft skills. Being professionally aware can help every individual in making wise decisions regarding their professional life and thus make the best out of their skills and interests. So, come join us from 4th to 6th November 2022 to accentuate a brighter and more excelled version of you for the future of a better living world.
