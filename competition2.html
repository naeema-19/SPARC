MOCK INTERVIEW

  • Date : 10thOctober

To help students gain confidence in their verbal and non-verbal communication abilities, the mock interview is yet another competition where participants shall be judged based on their skills to effectively communicate their point of view for the questions asked.

All phases of the competition will be held completely online on the 12th of October 2022.

Guidelines

  • The competition is conducted in online mode.
  • The competition will be conducted in 2 rounds.
  • Certificates will be provided to the participants who complete round 1.
  • Last date of registration: 5th October 2022
  • All further updates will be announced in the WhatsApp group (participants will be added to the group by the organizers).

Round 1

  • In this round, participants will have to complete an “Aptitude Test” (the date and time will be announced later in the group).
  • The test will be conducted through a google form.
  • The test will contain 25 questions and participants have to complete the test within 30 minutes from the starting time.
  • Only the shortlisted participants will be allowed to participate in the second round.

Round 2

  • In this round participants will go through a live interview for a job position given by the interviewers.
  • The interviewers will give the participants the job position they have to prepare for 1 day before the interview.
  • Participants will have to submit their resumes at the time of the interview.
  • Each interview will be 15 minutes long.
  • Winners will be chosen on the basis of the creativity and justification of their answers and also based on their knowledge of certain aspects of the job.
