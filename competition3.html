PHOTO MANIPULATION

  • Date : 10thOctober

The field of photography has transformed in the past decades, and along with it, we have seen the various tools that have caused this transformation. To improve a photograph according to our needs comprises various techniques and methods which we need to know.

Photo Manipulation will test the various skills that you have as a professional photographer to transform photos into creative works of art, which will make them stand out from the crowd. The competition will be held online on the 15th of October, 2022.
