Dr.Anil Lamba

Dr.Anil Lamba, a Chartered Accountant by profession, is the director of Lamcon Finance & Management Services Pvt Ltd. Being the author of the bestselling books ‘Romancing the Balance Sheet,’ ‘Eye on the Bottom Line,’ and ‘Financial Affairs of the Common Man.’, Dr.Lamba is an international corporate trainer whose international training programs are held across the USA, Russia, Africa, Middle East and the Far- East. Moreover, he is also a financial literacy activist who has actively worked in the field of eLearning and online training through his videos and talk sessions.
